Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take? ⬇️

We typically provide a cash offer within 24 hours of submitting your information. Once you accept our offer, we can close in as little as 7-14 days, depending on your timeline and any legal requirements.

Do you buy houses in any condition? ⬇️

Yes! We buy houses as-is, whether they need repairs or not. You don't need to worry about fixing anything up. We handle all the repairs and renovations after purchase.

Are there any hidden fees or commissions? ⬇️

No. We believe in complete transparency. Our offer is what you get at closing. There are no agent commissions, closing costs, or hidden fees. What we offer is what you keep.

What if I still have a mortgage? ⬇️

No problem! We can pay off your existing mortgage from the sale proceeds. You'll walk away with the remaining cash in your pocket.

How do you determine your offer price? ⬇️

We evaluate the property's condition, location, current market value, and potential for improvement. Our goal is to make you a fair offer quickly while accounting for our renovation costs and market conditions.

Is there any obligation to accept your offer? ⬇️

Not at all! Getting our offer is completely free and comes with no obligation. Review it, compare it to other options, and decide what's best for you.
